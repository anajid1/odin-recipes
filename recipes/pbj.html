Peanut Butter and Jelly Sandwich

Description

A pb&j sandwich is a quick, simple, and delicious meal.

Ingredients

  • 2 slices of bread
  • 2 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 2 tablespoons of you choice of jelly (grape is a safe option)

Steps

  1. Spread peanut butter on 1 side of a slice bread.
  2. Spread jelly on 1 side of the other slice of bread.
  3. Gently press these 2 slices making sure that the peanut butter and jelly sides are the ones making contact.
  4. Enjoy!
