Scrambled Egg

Description

Simple scrambled egg recipe; great for breakfast!

Ingredients

  • 4 eggs
  • 2 tablespoons of butter
  • 2 teaspoons of salt
  • 2 teaspoons of pepper

Steps

  1. Crack for 4 eggs into a pot.
  2. Add butter to pot.
  3. Put pot over medium-low heat.
  4. Using a rubber spatula continually keep mixing the egg in smooth circular motions.
  5. When egg starts to solidify add the salt and pepper.
  6. Continue cooking and mixing egg till its at consistency slightly less than what you'd like. Keep in mind egg continues to cook even when you turn off heat.
  7. Quickly put egg into a plate (to stop it from overcooking) and by then egg should have cooked to a level you like.
  8. Enjoy!
