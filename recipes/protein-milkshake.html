Protein Milkshake

Description

A quick, nutritious snack for anyone trying to get in a large protein intake.

Ingredients

  • 1 banana
  • 100 grams of oats
  • 30 grams of chia seeds
  • 4 ice cubes
  • 1 cup of almond milk
  • 4 spoons of plain whole greek yogurt
  • Handful of spinach
  • 4 tablespoons of peanut butter
  • 4 teaspoons of sugar (optional)

Steps

  1. Put all these ingredients in a blender.
  2. Blend it together till you get a consistency you like.
  3. Cheers!
